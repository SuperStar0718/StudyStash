F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I access the Study-Stash program?

    To access Study-Stash, you need to create an account and subscribe to a membership plan. Click on the "Get Started" button to begin the registration process. Make sure to have your school ID and other supporting documents ready, as they may be required during the registration process.

  • What are the pricing options for membership plans?

    We offer two types of membership plans: Normal and Pro. The Normal plan is priced at $20 and provides access to cash rewards and eligibility to apply for scholarships and grants. The Pro plan is priced at $40 and offers access to all types of rewards provided by Study-Stash.

  • How can I apply for loans?

    To be eligible for loans applications, you must meet the following criteria:

    • Be a registered member of Study-Stash.
    • Subscribe to the Pro plan.
    The loans application window is open only during the months when schools are opening. If you miss this period, you will need to wait until the next application window opens.

  • What kind of rewards can I expect as a member?

    As a member of Study-Stash, you can earn a diverse range of rewards based on your participation. These rewards include cash incentives, scholarships, grants, school utensils like laptops, tablets, books, exercise-books, clothes, and many others. The rewards are designed to motivate and support your academic journey and personal development.

  • Does the scholarship funds go directly to the student or the school?

    The scholarship funds awarded through Study-Stash are sent directly to the concerned school. The funds are intended to support the student's education and are managed by the school to ensure they are used for their intended purpose.

  • Does Study-Stash support students in finding job opportunities?

    Yes, at Study-Stash, we are dedicated to supporting students in their career journeys. We are proud to be sponsored by the "FCW Corporation", which works tirelessly to create valuable career opportunities for our members.

    As a Study-Stash member, you'll have access to exclusive job listings, internship opportunities, and career development resources to help you thrive in the job market. Our mission is to empower students with the skills and connections they need to succeed in their careers.

    Join Study-Stash today to take advantage of our career support and unlock a world of opportunities!

  • How can I participate in answering research and survey questions?

    Study-Stash encourages student collaboration and engagement. You can actively participate by answering research and survey questions that help in solving community problems. These questions will be provided within the platform for you to contribute your insights and ideas.

  • What courses are available on Study-Stash?

    Explore a diverse array of courses with Study-Stash. While we strive to cover a wide range of disciplines from universities worldwide, your desired course might not be listed yet. Don't be discouraged â€“ our course list is regularly updated. Check back for new additions. For the current list, visit "Courses we Support" in your workspace. We're committed to a comprehensive learning experience tailored to your interests.

  • What if I don't see my school listed during registration?

    If you don't see your school listed during the registration process, there's no need to worry! Our school list is continuously updated on a daily basis to include new institutions.

    We strive to expand our reach and include as many schools as possible to provide support to all students. To stay informed about the latest updates and school additions, we encourage you to subscribe to our newsletter.

    By subscribing to our newsletter, you'll receive regular updates on new school additions and other exciting opportunities. Once your school is listed, you can join and register as a member of Study-Stash to access our wide range of benefits and rewards.

    Don't miss out on being a part of Study-Stash! Subscribe to our newsletter today and be the first to know when your school is added.

  • Are project presentations only limited to school projects?

    At Study-Stash, we believe in nurturing talent and supporting students beyond their school projects. While we do offer opportunities for project presentations during our annual event in August, our support extends beyond academic projects.

    If you are working on any innovative project, whether it's a personal project, startup idea, or community initiative, we encourage you to showcase it to our community. Our platform is open to all members, and we provide a space to present your projects and receive valuable feedback and recognition.

    Whether it's a tech-related project, social initiative, or artistic endeavor, Study-Stash is here to help you shine. Our community values innovation and creativity, and we are excited to support your journey beyond the classroom.

    Don't miss the chance to showcase your talents and ideas! Join Study-Stash, be part of our growing community, and let your projects reach new heights.
